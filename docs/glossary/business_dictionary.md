# ORVION Business Dictionary

Status: Draft

Purpose

The Business Dictionary is the single source of truth for:

- Business entities
- Enumerations
- Dropdown values
- Status values
- Business codes
- Business terminology
- Naming standards

Rules

- No duplicate business terms.
- One canonical name for every business concept.
- lower_snake_case for all fixed values.
- Every table references the Business Dictionary.
- Every API references the Business Dictionary.
- Every UI dropdown references the Business Dictionary.
- Every report references the Business Dictionary.

Nothing may exist outside this document.
